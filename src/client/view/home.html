Thank you for your interest in tellem. This is a demonstration of the core functionality of tellem - real-time, channel-based notifications.

About tellem

In the side-bar you'll see a list of channels. Channels are a way of categorizing bulletins. When you want to send a bulletin, you specify which channel to send it through. Only users subscribed to that channel will receive the bulletin.

You can try this now by subscribing to either dev_ops or new_clients, and waiting for a bulletin to be sent. To see more information and subscribe to a channel, click its name in the sidebar.

Try creating some of your own channels and sending your own notifications.

Security Notice

The demo is currently running on development infrastructure, so avoid sending any personal data via tellem for the moment. Besides that, feel free to play around.
